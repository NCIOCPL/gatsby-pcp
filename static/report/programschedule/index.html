How to Join the Innovation in Cancer Screening Meeting

Share:

VBRICK LINK (For viewers only): https://nci.rev.vbrick.com/#/webcasts/presidentscancerpanel

Below you will find instructions to send to all the attendees viewing from the Vbrick.

Attendee’s Instructions:

We recommend that you join at least 10 minutes before the meeting begins.

PLEASE NOTE: We recommend disconnecting from VPN if you are an NCI or NIH viewer, as it can result in a better streaming experience. If you are a viewer who works in an environment where VPN is being utilized, this may also better your streaming experience.

Instructions on how to view the Event:

NCI Staff:

We recommend disconnecting from the VPN and closing other running applications on your computer. You may also watch this webcast using your smartphone.

  1. Click on the Vbrick link provided.
  2. NCI Staff, enter the site as a “registered user.” Located on the bottom of the page.

If you are not an NCI Staff, follow these instructions below:

We recommend disconnecting from the VPN and closing other running applications on your computer. You may also watch this webcast using your smartphone.

  1. Click on the Vbrick link provided.
  2. Enter your first and last name in “Display Name” field.
  3. Enter your email in “Email” field.
  4. Check the consent/authorization box.
  5. Sign in as guest.

To Submit a Question during the meeting:

  1. Select the Q&A icon on the right.
  2. Type your question in the Question field.
  3. If enabled, select the Post Anonymously checkbox if you want the question to be anonymous.
  4. Click Submit.

To Chat during the meeting:

  1. Select the chat icon on the right.
  2. Type your comment.
  3. Click Submit.

Vbrick Live stream controls:

  • Click the information tab to close it. This is indicated on the right of the screen by the letter “I.”
  • Select “unmute” in the upper menu panel of the event window to hear the streamed audio from your computer speakers. Use your computer to adjust the volume.
  • For Full screen, select the far-right icon within the video to enlarge the presentation window.
  • A recording will be made available to the Vbrick site, a link will be provided later.
